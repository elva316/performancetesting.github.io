Fair Weather Travel

(Tech: Angular & Express)

Fair-Weather-Travel is an Angular web app that allows travelers and tourists to check the weather forecast of their destinations. The weather data are fetched from external OpenWeatherMap APIs; which are then parsed and displayed to user. Users also can read travel relevant blogs and RSS feeds.

This app is deployed on Heroku server.

(Tech: Angular & Express & mongoDB)

ThriftShop Marketplace is a full MEAN web app that allows users to sign-up to buy and sell used products. Logged-in users can post items they wish to sell and perform full CRUD operations. Also, users can leave comments or feedback on an item, and choose to like or dislike it.
